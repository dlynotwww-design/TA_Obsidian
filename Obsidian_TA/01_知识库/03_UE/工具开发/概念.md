### 编辑器工具 (Editor Utilities) - 最常见的语境

这是Unreal中提及“Utility”最普遍的场景，核心是**用于在编辑器环境下自动化工作流、处理资产或辅助开发的一系列蓝图工具**，以前常被称为“Blutility”[](https://dev.epicgames.com/documentation/unreal-engine/scripting-the-editor-using-blueprints?application_version=4.27&lang=pt-BR)[](https://dev.epicgames.com/documentation/unreal-engine/scripted-actions-in-unreal-engine?application_version=5.6)。

- **核心概念**：通过创建特殊的“编辑器工具蓝图 (Editor Utility Blueprint)”，你可以编写在编辑器中运行的逻辑，用以批量处理任务、快速修改场景或扩展编辑器菜单[](https://dev.epicgames.com/documentation/unreal-engine/scripting-the-editor-using-blueprints?application_version=4.27&lang=pt-BR)[](https://dev.epicgames.com/documentation/unreal-engine/scripted-actions-in-unreal-engine?application_version=5.6)。
    
- **主要类型与功能**：
    
    - **Scripted Actions (脚本化动作)**：最常用的形式。可以创建右键菜单命令，直接对选中的**资产 (Asset)** 或** Actor (场景中的实例)** 执行操作。例如，在内容浏览器中选中多个模型，右键一键完成三角面数简化[](https://dev.epicgames.com/documentation/unreal-engine/create-action-utilities-with-geometry-scripting--in-unreal-engine?application_version=5.8)[](https://dev.epicgames.com/documentation/unreal-engine/scripted-actions-in-unreal-engine?application_version=5.6)[](https://dev.epicgames.com/documentation/unreal-engine/scripted-actions?application_version=4.27)。
        
    - **Editor Utility Widget (编辑器工具控件)**：如果你需要一个带按钮、输入框等图形界面的自定义面板，可以通过 UMG 创建，并像其他编辑器面板一样停靠在UE界面中[](https://dev.epicgames.com/documentation/unreal-engine/scripting-the-editor-using-blueprints?application_version=4.27&lang=pt-BR)。
        
    - **Editor Utility Task (编辑器异步任务)**：用于执行耗时较长的批量操作（如批量生成贴图），避免界面卡死[](https://bbs.huaweicloud.com/blogs/427659)。
        
- **关键用法**：
    
    - **限制作用对象**：可以在蓝图类默认设置中指定支持的类，让右键菜单只在特定类型（如 `StaticMeshActor`）上才显示，避免误操作[](https://dev.epicgames.com/documentation/unreal-engine/create-action-utilities-with-geometry-scripting--in-unreal-engine?application_version=5.8)[](https://dev.epicgames.com/documentation/unreal-engine/scripted-actions-in-unreal-engine?application_version=5.6)。
        
    - **动态输入**：可以为脚本化动作添加输入参数，在右键点击时会弹出窗口让用户填写具体数值，非常灵活[](https://dev.epicgames.com/documentation/unreal-engine/create-action-utilities-with-geometry-scripting--in-unreal-engine?application_version=5.8)[](https://dev.epicgames.com/documentation/unreal-engine/scripted-actions-in-unreal-engine?application_version=5.6)。
